Professional Team

Why Book with us?

Our group, first called Gruppo Eurovisa, was created in 1968, to work initially as a tour operator specialized in coastline destinations, and suddenly focused on the cruise market and needs of the tourists due to the Cruise Industry development. Nowadays, our team counts with up to 30 experts, specialized in each area of our customer interests, and offer a range of services. Through its website, Cruisingrome, www.cruisingrome.com will inspire travellers to explore Italy's boundless possibilities. We are committed to working with all travel agencies and strenghten partners to improve customer service and choice!

Port Assistant

Our team is dedicated to create the best vacation experience for you! From the moment you arrive at Civitavechia's Port, we are in your disposition, giving you all the assistance you require related to the town, what to do, where to go, etc. Just look for the staff, wearing a white shirt with the Cruisingrome logo on it, and solve all your doubts!

Finest Selection

Cruisingrome is always seeking new ways to exceed our client's expectations. We constantly update the services to offer you interesting and special deals. We experience and visit personaly the places listed on the Tours section. Have a look in our Trip Gallery to see some photos taken during our stay! Everything we do is designed with you in mind! We just select what would suit your needs during your best period of the year!

World of choise

You don't need to spend more time on the web searching for the best deals for transfer, hotel, luggage delivery/deposit or city tours during your stay. Cruisingrome has a whole world of choice! We have very exciting vacation packages to you! Just in case it's difficult to choose, call us or send us an e-mail and we can tailor your vacation in every detail, with your budget in mind. We are here to give you inspiration and take care of all your bookings!

Best available price

Cruisingrome believes that incredible travel memories might be priceless, but we are satisfied to know you got a great deal. We maintain a good relationship with our suppliers to ensure the services on our website have the best avaiable price. If we can find a way to save cost, we pass it down to our customers..

Multilangual Experts

Our experienced multilingual team is made of native speakers in English, Spanish, Portuguese, Russian and German, so there won't be any confusion in planning your trip. We are at disposal of our clients, providing only the finest solutions in the travel industry. We are always eager to learn in order to make every customer happy!

Sure Touch

We are passionately tourism specialists with 40 years of experience in this sector. Our passion is to give you commodity during your deserved vacation, in order to make you relax and enjoy every moment of your trip without any worries, offering you all the assistance you need, from the moment you leave home to the moment you come back

Privacy

Cruisingrome cares about the privacy of your database. Your personal information (name, postal address and telephone number) will only be used to guarantee your booking and to enable us to contact you about the service you have requested.

Safe and Easy Booking

When you book with Cruisingrome your information is protected under a secure connection. You will find true avaiability and exact price, according to the period of the year and the number of people. All you have to do is to click BOOK NOW. In case you don't want to book online, just ring us and we can take care of every step of your trip. We are an agency IATA and through cooperation with trusted partners on site, your vacation is financially protected.

Rest assured

When you book with Cruisingrome, nothing is left to chance. You can use our Luggage Delivery if you want to travel with no bags or can ask us for a transfer if you need to go to Rome or the surrounding areas, or even to another region of Italy with commodity! You may prefer use the Sightseeing Bus to experience the magical sights of the city, or going by bus if you are in a big group, for example. We assure your rest by providing all the services you require!
