    Overview

    A guided sightseeing and walking tour to admire the main highlights of Rome in a very comfortable bus. You'll have 5 hours to find out why it is considered the Eternal City!! Enjoy your day! BOOK NOW!

    What you can expect:

    BOOK NOW

    Our private, comfortable and Luxury Bus depart from Civitavecchia Cruise Port at 9:30 am, that will drive along the highway towards the Eternal City. By 11:00 a.m. we should be there and then we begin our tour on foot walking through a very narrow way that suddenly will surprise you with its majestic and speechless Trevi's Fountain. This world's famous landmark is surrounded by tourists from all over the world trying to take the classical picture tossing a coin back, so that it guarantees your return to this magical city! We will then continue towards Venice Square that best represents the evolution of styles from the late 19th century to the era of Mussolini.You will also see the monument that contrasts with the rest of the city, Vittorio Emanuele II. On your left there's the detailed Trajan's Column, located in Trajan's Forum, built near the Quirinal Hill, north of the Roman Forum, where the Romans developed their social life. The view of the Palatine and the Roman Forum plunges us into the heart of another era. Just few steps away stands the absolutely breathtaking Colosseum, the main symbol of the eternity of the Roman civilization, able to hold up to 50.000 spectators, eager to shows such as fighting animals, gladiators, and even naval battles! In this area we will be able to admire the magnificent Arch of Constantine. After such beauty, we'll take you in a restaurant, where you can delight the typical Italian food. The next point to visit is the imposing St. Peter's Basilica, located in the Vatican city and one of the most visited places in Rome. The last spot is a surprise view offered by the bus driver of Cruisingrome. Are you curious? BOOK NOW! At 4.00 o'clock, we will return to Civitavecchia, making sure you have enjoyed every moment of this unique experience!!
